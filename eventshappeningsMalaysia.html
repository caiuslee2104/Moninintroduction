Malaysia Monin Cup 2016

The Malaysia Monin Cup 2016 is a competition that took place in Malaysia, where participants demonstrated their skills in mixology. Monin, known for its syrups and flavoring products, sponsors this event. The competition typically includes bartenders and baristas showcasing their creativity and expertise in crafting unique beverages using Monin products.


Malaysia Monin Cup 2022

The Malaysia Monin Cup 2022 saw a variety of talented mixologists showcasing their skills. One notable winner was Mark Defensor from The Spirits Library in the Philippines, who crafted a cocktail called "Bicol Speedster," inspired by the Filipino dish Bicol Express. His creation included ingredients like Tanduay Asian Gold Rum, coconut milk, Pork Fatwashed Habanero Lime Liqueur, and Monin Ginger Syrup, garnished with siling labuyo (chili pepper) and black pepper. 

Malaysia FHA 2022

The Food and Hotel Malaysia (FHM) 2022 event took place from March 29 to April 1 at the Kuala Lumpur Convention Centre. This edition marked the 16th iteration of the trade-only food and hospitality exhibition, bringing together 500 participating brands and companies and attracting around 8,000 trade buyers. Key highlights of FHM 2022 included plant-based food demonstrations, Industry 4.0 innovations, biodegradable products, and a focus on hygienic products and services. The event also featured live cooking demonstrations by celebrity chefs and the return of Culinaire Malaysia, which showcased the skills of over 1,000 culinary professionals in various competitions.
